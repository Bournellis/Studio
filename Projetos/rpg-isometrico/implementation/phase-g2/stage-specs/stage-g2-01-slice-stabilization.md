# Stage G2-01 - Slice Stabilization

## Goal

Harden the first playable Godot slice into a stable baseline for continued migration work.

## Required Outcome

- the frontend remains usable and legible at common desktop window sizes
- the arena camera and world framing are consistently readable
- combat feedback is easier to judge and iterate on
- the main scenes are easier to reason about and maintain
- automated validation protects the baseline from obvious regressions

## Scope

- frontend UX hardening
- arena readability and camera tuning
- combat presentation and clarity passes
- scene/runtime cleanup
- validation and smoke improvements

## Non-Goals

- more content breadth
- online features
- mobile support
- progression systems
