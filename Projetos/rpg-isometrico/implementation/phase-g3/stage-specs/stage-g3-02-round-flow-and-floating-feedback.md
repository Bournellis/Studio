# Stage G3-02 - Round Flow And Floating Feedback

## Goal

Make the round rhythm and on-hit readability easier to understand at a glance.

## Required Outcome

- the round has a readable pre-match countdown instead of dropping into combat abruptly
- the end of the round has a short readable beat before the result overlay takes over
- floating feedback appears for damage, healing, barrier, block, and death moments
- automated validation protects the updated session flow and arena presentation anchors

## Scope

- explicit round countdown
- explicit post-match linger
- floating combat feedback text
- session flow validation

## Non-Goals

- new skills or races
- progression expansion
- online features
- mobile controls
