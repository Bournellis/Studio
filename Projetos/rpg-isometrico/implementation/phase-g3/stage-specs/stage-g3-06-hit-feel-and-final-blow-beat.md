# Stage G3-06 - Hit Feel And Final Blow Beat

## Goal

Make hits land with more weight and give the final blow a stronger beat before the result overlay.

## Required Outcome

- targets react more clearly when damage connects
- short hit-stop motion pauses give exchanges a more solid feel without destabilizing the slice
- the final blow breathes slightly longer before the result overlay appears
- automated validation protects the new combat feedback hooks

## Scope

- stronger target-side impact visuals
- short shared motion pause on hit and block moments
- slightly longer post-match linger
- tests for impact feedback and motion-pause hooks

## Non-Goals

- new combat systems
- balance tuning
- content breadth expansion
- online or mobile work
