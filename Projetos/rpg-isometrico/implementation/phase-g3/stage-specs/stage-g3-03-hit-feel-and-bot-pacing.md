# Stage G3-03 - Hit Feel And Bot Pacing

## Goal

Make combat impacts feel stronger and the bot rhythm easier to read without adding new content breadth.

## Required Outcome

- player hits and damage exchanges feel more present through camera response
- the HUD surfaces basic attack and dash readiness more clearly
- the bot no longer sticks to a flat chase pattern and shows a clearer reposition rhythm
- automated validation protects the new pacing and impact hooks

## Scope

- arena camera impact feedback
- cooldown readability for core actions
- bot reposition and pacing rhythm
- validation updates for the new combat feel baseline

## Non-Goals

- new skills or races
- progression expansion
- online features
- mobile controls
