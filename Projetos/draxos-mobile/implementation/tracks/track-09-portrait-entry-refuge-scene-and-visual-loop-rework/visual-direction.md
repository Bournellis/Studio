# Track 09 Visual Direction

## North Star

DraxosMobile should feel like a portrait mobile game, not a debug dashboard. The first playable home is the Refugio: a magical altar scene where the player chooses actions through objects, icons and hotspots layered over a scene.

Hero Wars is a useful structural reference for density and hotspot composition, but DraxosMobile should keep its own tone: magic, altar, ritual, resources and async battle preparation.

## Entry

Entry is operational and should be comfortable:

- Login/create account are primary actions.
- Save normal/lab is chosen before entering the playable home.
- Reset/sync/update live in secondary areas.
- Dev labs are visible only in dev/editor contexts.
- The main CTA is `Entrar no Refugio`.

Entry is not the Refugio and should not pretend to be the game scene.

## Refugio

Refugio is the playable home:

- The altar is the main visual anchor.
- Resources and status live at the top.
- Hotspots open Battle, Base management, Social, Competition, Shop and Profile.
- Menus open full or partial internal screens with Back.
- The current Base systems become Refugio management, not the first screen.

## Battle

Battle is fullscreen portrait for this phase:

- No app chrome during battle running or summary.
- The stage is vertical.
- `Pular` is a large action in the bottom portion of the layout.
- Summary keeps winner, duration, events, reward, resources and return/replay/history actions.

## Reference Images

`assets/referenciaimagens/` is a moodboard/reference folder. Images there can guide composition, scale, mood and future art requests, but they are not runtime assets in Track 09.

Future asset tracks should convert this direction into explicit slots, ids, dimensions and fallback rules before importing final art.
