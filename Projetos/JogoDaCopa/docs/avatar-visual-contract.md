# JogoDaCopa Avatar Visual Contract

The current avatar system is a runtime primitive placeholder for football minigames.

## Current Contract

- Avatars are presentation-only.
- Skin tone and country-inspired kit selection are memory-only.
- No official country logos or licensed uniform replicas.
- Player and bot must remain readable from the third-person camera.
- Animation states are basic: idle, move, jump, fall, kick, strong kick, celebrate and hit.

## Future Direction

When the game needs higher visual quality, replace or augment this procedural avatar with authored/AI-assisted assets behind the same presentation contract.
