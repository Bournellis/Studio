# JogoDaCopa Tuning Guide

Tune in this order:

1. Third-person camera distance, height and ball focus.
2. Player movement speed and turning feel.
3. Ball mass, friction, bounce and kick force.
4. Possession radius, dribble strength and kick assist radius.
5. Bot approach offset, kick cadence and defend/attack switching.
6. HUD and intro readability.

Do not tune FPS weapons, arena pickups or shooter bot behavior here. `FpsPlayground` owns those systems.
