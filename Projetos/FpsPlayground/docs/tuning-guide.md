# FpsPlayground Tuning Guide

Tune in this order:

1. Player movement and mouse sensitivity.
2. Rifle/Plasma Bolt damage, cooldown and knockback.
3. Bot shot pressure and aim fairness.
4. Pickup respawn and route value.
5. Arena cover, jump pad and high-route readability.

Do not tune football values here. `JogoDaCopa` owns football possession, ball physics and TPS camera tuning.
